# COFFEE-SHOP-SALES-ANALYSIS

This project analyzes sales data from a coffee shop to uncover insights about customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends
the objectives of Analyze daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends.
Identify best-selling products and categories.
Understand customer purchasing patterns and peak sales hours.
Evaluate the impact of promotions or discounts on revenue.
Provide actionable recommendations for business growth.
the project used tools and tecnologies are involves python, excel, power bi 
the visualization for hour,monthly,and daily sales items Best selling products and categories
Top revenue-generating items Year over year or month over month growth 
Insights and Recommendations for this projects
Identify top-performing products and focus marketing on them.
Adjust staffing and inventory during peak hours.
Introduce bundle offers for low performing products.
Consider loyalty programs to encourage repeat customers.
The goal is to identify patterns that can help improve business strategies, increase sales, and optimize inventory management.
