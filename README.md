# COFFEE-SHOP-SALES-ANALYSIS
This project analyzes sales data from a coffee shop to uncover insights about customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.
The goal is to identify patterns that can help improve business strategies, increase sales, and optimize inventory management.

Objectives

Analyze daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends.
Identify best-selling products and top-performing categories.
Understand customer purchasing patterns and peak sales hours.
Evaluate the impact of promotions or discounts on revenue.
Provide actionable recommendations for business growth.

Tools and Technologies
Python – Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis
Excel – Data review and summary statistics
Power BI – Interactive dashboards and data visualization

Visualizations
Hourly, Daily, and Monthly Sales Trends
Best-Selling Products and Categories
Top Revenue-Generating Items

Insights and Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products.
Adjust staffing and inventory during peak sales hours.
Introduce bundle offers for low-performing items.
Implement loyalty programs to encourage repeat customers.
